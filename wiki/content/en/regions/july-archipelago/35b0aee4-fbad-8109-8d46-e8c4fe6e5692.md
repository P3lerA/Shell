The archipelago on the western side of Ragriff, where the {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81c7-bef2-fa4750d2e5b7|Althea}} regime is located, has close exchanges with the Ragriff mainland.

&nbsp;

When the first elves landed on it, time flowed to July of a certain year during the Sprouting Period, and thus the July Archipelago received its name. From this, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81c7-bef2-fa4750d2e5b7|Althea}} began.
