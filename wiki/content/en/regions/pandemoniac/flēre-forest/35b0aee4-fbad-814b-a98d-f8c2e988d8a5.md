A forest located in the southern reaches of Pandemoniac, rarely visited by anyone. Looking from the north bank of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81fc-96e1-cb45c169a26d|Lukatýsrika}}, one can see that the flēre Forest has rain throughout the entire year.

However, inside the forest there is no sign of rain — only faint weeping sounds can be heard. This is how the flēre Forest got its name.
