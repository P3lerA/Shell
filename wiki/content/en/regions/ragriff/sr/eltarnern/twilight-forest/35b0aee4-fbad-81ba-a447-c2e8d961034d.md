A forest in the northern part of Eltarnern, named for its majestic trees that block out the sky, leaving only the faint glow of fireflies visible.

&nbsp;

When the Twilight Forest was {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-813f-99bd-d0a2ab1ac81e|The Pale Night}}, the elves who migrated north were formed from the bodies of the martyred to prevent the spread of pallorization.
