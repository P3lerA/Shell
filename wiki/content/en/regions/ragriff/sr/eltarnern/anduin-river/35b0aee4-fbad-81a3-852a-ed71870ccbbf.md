The long river that originates from {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d9-9bba-e7a4c550da69|Mountain System Dol Guidor}}, its upper reaches are generally referred to as the section from {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-819e-8714-d1d33be9a203|Eltarnern}} to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ba-a447-c2e8d961034d|Twilight Forest}}.

Due to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-813f-99bd-d0a2ab1ac81e|The Pale Night}}, the upstream river water is entirely white and contains deadly poison.

Flowing northward, elves once migrated along its course.
