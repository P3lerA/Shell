*The Tree* of the elves, located within the city of Golhertarna. In the elvish tongue it means *Tree of Prophecy*, and is a blessing from the Textor Fati {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d9-a735-dc5e0d48876d|Mnemosyne}} to the elves in the distant age of antiquity.

&nbsp;

Guiding the fate of the elven race until it faded to pallor, now existing only in the elves' currency and insignias.

&nbsp;

Commonly noted as *the Tree.*
