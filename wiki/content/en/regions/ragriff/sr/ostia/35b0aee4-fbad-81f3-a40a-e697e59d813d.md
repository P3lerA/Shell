A dwarven kingdom that originated in ancient times, located in the western part of South Ragriff, though scattered here and there with a few human settlements.

&nbsp;

Ostia's underground is filled with mines and fortresses, where dwarves display their innate talents.

&nbsp;

The capital is located at {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8104-8b38-d899cdacfcc6|Erebor}}, and Ostian dialect is spoken throughout the territory, a variant of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a0-be0f-ff62f4ed24ba|Lingua-Franca}}.
