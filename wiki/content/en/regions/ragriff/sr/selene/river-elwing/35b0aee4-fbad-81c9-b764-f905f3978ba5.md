A river located in southern Ragriff, flowing through Selene; a major tributary of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ac-b42f-efb77c4d7e4d|Anduin Downstream}}. Elwing means *scattered starlight*.

When the northward-migrating Elves passed by, they saw the starlight reflected in the water, and thus the River Elwing got its name.
