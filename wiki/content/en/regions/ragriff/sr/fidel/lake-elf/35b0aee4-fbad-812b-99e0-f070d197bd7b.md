Located in the northern part of South Ragriff, within the territory of Fidel. In the elven language, **ELf** means *Lake of the Elves*.

The lake water is clear, teeming with aquatic life.

The displaced {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81e3-b34f-f38d0c851cf9|High Elf}} who had migrated north finally stopped their wandering, swearing to restore the glory of the elven race, and thus the lake came to be called Lake Elf.
