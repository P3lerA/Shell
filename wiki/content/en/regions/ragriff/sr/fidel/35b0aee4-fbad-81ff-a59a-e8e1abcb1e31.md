Fidel (Elvish: **Phèdre**) is an ancient elf empire located in the northern Ragriff, governed by {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-811c-bdb9-f23f6e564acc|Clan Elizabeth}}. Its rulers are elected internally through the empire, making it a nation with advanced magic and a long history.

&nbsp;

Its capital is located in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8118-acf7-c8486b182532|Olympia}}, and its official language is {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ff-a3b4-cd4d2b6ed5c6|Elvish}}.

&nbsp;

Fidel originated in the {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-819e-8714-d1d33be9a203|Eltarnern}} , the southernmost part of Ragriff that has since become wasteland. After {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-813f-99bd-d0a2ab1ac81e|The Pale Night}}, the elves migrated north to where Fidel now resides. Most {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d9-856a-db25779096a2|Common Elf}} migrated further north to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8109-8d46-e8c4fe6e5692|July Archipelago}}.
