A long river rising from {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8197-8ef4-c38aa8d43b86|Mount Angro}}, nurturing the towns on both banks.

&nbsp;

The river flows southeast until it empties into {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-819e-858f-ff02327c8aac|Lake Luna}} in the territory of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-818d-956e-e2a7a7b1329d|Selene}}.
