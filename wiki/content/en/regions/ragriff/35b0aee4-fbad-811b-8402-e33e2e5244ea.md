The name of the main continent in this world-setting. Generally, this name is used when referring to the entire world.

&nbsp;

A continent located at the center of the world. As for why it's the center of the world — because the world is flat. Have you ever seen a fictional continent on a spherical surface?

&nbsp;

In fact, there are other continents in the eastern reaches of the world as well, but they are far smaller than Ragriff, and because they were sparsely inhabited during the era of the story, they are rarely mentioned.
