A small northern nation of the Southern Confederacy, birthplace of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-818d-80c3-c1c7f0b0045f|ALLGOES}}. Once the largest nation in northern Argos's heartland, present-day Bellum is the remaining territory left after {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8136-9faf-c037fd931a68|Argos}}'s independence.

&nbsp;

The word Bellum comes from Latin, meaning "war".
