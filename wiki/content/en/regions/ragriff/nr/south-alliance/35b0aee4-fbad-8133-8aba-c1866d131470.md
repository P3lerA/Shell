A loose federation located in the southern part of Northern Ragriff, adjacent to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8136-9faf-c037fd931a68|Argos}}.

In the past, the small nations that had fought against each other formed an unstable alliance at the start of the Rejuvenation Era, yet deep-seated contradictions among them persisted.

There is no de jure capital, and {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a0-be0f-ff62f4ed24ba|Lingua-Franca}} is used throughout the territory.
