Located in the far east of the Between Lands, this small town on the edge of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8109-825c-d1e07b11d52d|Foggy Forest}} is frequented by all sorts of people. {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8163-a0d6-d76cd22fd2a2|M.A.R.}} has a branch here.

Because it is located near the Foggy Forest, the town is often shrouded in thick fog, which is how it got its name.
