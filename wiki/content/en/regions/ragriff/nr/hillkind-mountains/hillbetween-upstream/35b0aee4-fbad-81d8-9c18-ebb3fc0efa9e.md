Gushing from the valleys between steep mountain ranges, the upstream of Hillbetween Upstream — unlike the tranquil downstream — frequently presents spectacular waterfalls.

&nbsp;

After flowing out of the mountains, it splits into two branches—one running southward through {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8136-9faf-c037fd931a68|Argos}}, while the one flowing westward toward {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8107-8d84-fe317ecf0276|SingLand}}, called {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-813e-b8ae-ff39f5011acf|Source River}} by the people of SingLand.

&nbsp;

Notably, "Hillbetween Upstream" is the name given by the Argosians. The Hillkind call it **Bendecir River**, meaning *blessing*.
