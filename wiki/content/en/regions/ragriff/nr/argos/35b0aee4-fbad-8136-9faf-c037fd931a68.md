Located in the northern part of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-811b-8402-e33e2e5244ea|Ragriff}}, it is an empire that gradually rose after gaining independence from {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81c8-b829-fbb57fb7f1a0|Bellum}} around 2,900 years ago, and is therefore also called "New Bellum."

&nbsp;

Its predecessor was an unnamed small town at the foot of the Hillkind Mountains (now the capital of Argos, also named Argos), which was saved by the young {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-818d-80c3-c1c7f0b0045f|ALLGOES}} and renamed Argos.

&nbsp;

Most residents make their living through farming, with no official language, generally using {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a0-be0f-ff62f4ed24ba|Lingua-Franca}}. The ancestors of the Argos people pursued the phantom of the god of war, and this became the origin of their united and resilient character.

&nbsp;

Since its formation, it has continuously sought outward expansion, making Argosians highly aggressive but also united and resilient.
