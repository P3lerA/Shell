Originating from {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-812a-9008-f5805e43ea39|Hillkind Mountains}} and flowing into {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81c1-b1d1-c937d000a66a|Green Lake}}, this river traverses the entire territory of Argos.

&nbsp;

When the river flows to the foot of the mountain, the flow rate gradually slows, allowing the Argosians to obtain abundant irrigation water sources.
