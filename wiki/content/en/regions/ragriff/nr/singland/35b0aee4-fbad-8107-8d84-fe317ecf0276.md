A multi-ethnic republic primarily consisting of Humans, located in the western part of North Ragriff and bordering Argos.

Its origin: A republic founded around the year 600 with {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-818b-a71e-cba6e166d7f7|Singhui}} as its capital, which gradually expanded over a century and finally unified the western part of North Ragriff in 1582 — this is today's SingLand.

&nbsp;

It is a culturally diverse, highly developed nation that values harmony. Its capital is located in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-818b-a71e-cba6e166d7f7|Singhui}}, and the {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a0-be0f-ff62f4ed24ba|Lingua-Franca}} is spoken throughout its territory.
