This woodland within SingLand, adjacent to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-813c-ae78-e8e2d19f392c|Singlake}}, has recently been opened to visitors. Within it are wondrous beasts of every kind.

The garden is roughly divided into a pine forest area, a bamboo forest area, a broadleaf forest area, and a flower garden area.
