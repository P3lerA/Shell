A wonder located in the southern part of Lukatýsrika, a city-state completely suspended in the air.

&nbsp;

Katafýgio was originally a peaceful ordinary city-state located beside {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81b0-985c-ea7e4599f6cf|River Yara}}. However, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8130-abfc-f1faa945a04f|Demon}} that migrated northward in large numbers during its formative period transformed the ordinary city into hell.

Some of the Katafýgio people who were unwilling to leave collected large amounts of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8137-b11d-ce9875dcfe03|Pierre de Couhard}} from {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8120-b502-f83362aabb04|Mountains of Yore}}, and by this means raised their homeland into the sky — such is the origin of the spectacle that is the floating city.
