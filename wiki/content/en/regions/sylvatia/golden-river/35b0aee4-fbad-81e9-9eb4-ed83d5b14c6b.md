The river originating from {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-814a-a2c9-e9f4d411c8a4|Platinum Ridge}} splits into two branches at the foot of the mountain.

One branch that flows through {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81bc-baef-cf3413a39925|Zeska}} leads to the route towards {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8133-8aba-c1866d131470|South Alliance}}, while the other flows towards the route to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81fc-96e1-cb45c169a26d|Lukatýsrika}} and {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8144-b705-c03ba8df8fdd|NR}}.

&nbsp;

The Golden River does not actually produce gold sand; rather, its superior location brought countless wealth to the cities along its banks, making Silvatia a nation of trade. This is how the Golden River earned its epithet.
