The so-called Guidance is when the Creator granted the god of wisdom ({{link:35b0aee4-fbad-810b-bfc0-c7412c6c5109|Daemon R. Brigid}}) a divine position to enlighten mortals. From then on, people gained the ability to think.

&nbsp;

General history also uses the Guidance as a dividing point; the period before the Guidance without clear chronology is called BG, meaning Before Guidance, while the period after is called AG, meaning After Guidance.
