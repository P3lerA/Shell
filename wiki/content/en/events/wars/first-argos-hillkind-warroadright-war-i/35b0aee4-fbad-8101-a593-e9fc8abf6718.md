{{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8136-9faf-c037fd931a68|Argos}} has been in long-term conflict with {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8142-bf13-f4676fc46608|Hillkind}} since its birth.

When Argos, as a newly emerging nation that had not yet stabilized, sought to expand outward, it cast its covetous eyes upon the ambiguously defined borderlands between {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-812a-9008-f5805e43ea39|Hillkind Mountains}} and Argos.

Being long plagued by the aggressive actions of Argos, the Hillkind initiated this war — the first conflict between the two. At that time, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-818d-80c3-c1c7f0b0045f|ALLGOES}} was still under the tutelage of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8156-adaa-e50b3b9d75a6|Ourea}}.
