{{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8198-9e06-c1a0920389c9|Munitio}}'s legate who carries out **fear**. They generally appear at the world's borders, such as {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8161-801f-c326dce0d0ba|Oceanus}}. They manifest in different forms depending on the circumstances, and their appearance generally triggers an uncontrollable, instinctive fear in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81df-b26a-e63ae871452a|Beings}}. For example, when someone tries to sail from the border sea toward the outer sea, when they travel too far, they can see what appears to be the wreckage of a sunken vessel—the description of someone who saw that thing, felt overwhelming terror, and turned back because of it. Most of those who continued forward never returned.
