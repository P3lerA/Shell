### Legates

{{link:35b0aee4-fbad-812e-b8de-d52deb2fc4e5|Legate}}

&nbsp;

In this world, Gods are more like rulers under Majesty, still retaining human desires.

Beings of a higher plane that more strongly manifest divinity, such as {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8156-adaa-e50b3b9d75a6|Ourea}}, are called Lord. They are generally products of the world compilation process itself, existing beyond human cognition, and what is seen in Ragriff is mostly their avatars.

Textor Fati are an even more abstract concept, more akin to tuners of causality, but not in any way perceptible to mortals.

The Legates act on behalf of the Avatars of Will, exercising abstract wills through concrete forms.
