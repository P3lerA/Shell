In this world, a year is also divided into twelve months.

Every three months is further divided into a season, and each season is governed by a {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8196-ac0f-c12f7b9e4ea4|Lord}}.

Spring [3,4,5] — {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-810f-be8c-d1fc29fa6d85|Aeolus}}

Summer[6,7,8] —{{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81e9-8853-c5321636d303|Hephaestus}}

Autumn [9,10,11] ——{{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8156-adaa-e50b3b9d75a6|Ourea}}

Winter[12,1,2] ——{{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81b5-ba56-ca91e07c56d9|Nereids}}

When the time points to a season governed by a certain Lord, it does not mean that they will become omnipotent. However, it is certain that their power will be greatly enhanced.
