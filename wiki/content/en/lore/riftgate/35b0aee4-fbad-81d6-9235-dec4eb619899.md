The transportation method closest to teleportation. Requires erecting a Riftgate frame between the origin and destination, with trained top-tier mages briefly opening the Riftgate on both sides. 

&nbsp;

Since the Mana needed to open the gate is staggering, and the frame itself requires considerable construction costs to withstand the immense pressure during opening, Riftgates have not been widely popularized in Ragriff.
