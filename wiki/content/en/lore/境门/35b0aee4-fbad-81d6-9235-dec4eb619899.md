The transportation method closest to teleportation. Requires erecting a 境门 frame between the origin and destination, with trained top-tier mages briefly opening the 境门 on both sides. Since the amount of magical power needed to open the door is staggering, and the door frame itself requires considerable construction costs to withstand the immense pressure during opening, 境门 has not been widely popularized in Ragriff.
