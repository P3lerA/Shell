Magic is the {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ec-bc80-e3542e080055|Element V}} in the {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-813b-aa3f-ed7ddd07602f|Four Elements}}, corresponding to aether in Plato's theory of five elements, and greatly affects the potency of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8190-89a7-e08879b4c098|Magic}} released by {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81df-b26a-e63ae871452a|Beings}}.

&nbsp;

Magic, like ether, is everywhere, so it can be perceived by beings with the ability to manipulate magic, and the strength of this perceptual ability has no direct relationship with the amount of magic (see below) (for {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ed-b1c0-d26f3c35e709|示踪术}}, however, there is a positive correlation, along with a unique mechanism). This means most magic leaves traces — traces that can be felt by {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8124-89bc-cca8ca5aa019|辛德拉试验}}. Additionally, there are methods like {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ed-b1c0-d26f3c35e709|示踪术}} and {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8124-89bc-cca8ca5aa019|辛德拉试验}} for visualizing the existence of magic.

Generally, unless it is a large-scale spell, traces become very weak one week after being released. For magicians without strong magic insight, they must carefully (actively) sense it to detect it. This is because free magic has the nature of equalizing, naturally flowing from areas of high concentration to low concentration like a gas. Even so, due to magic's adhesion effect on the surface it was released onto, even after several decades, as long as the surface of the affected object has not been treated with suppressants such as {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8154-b805-e96fa930e179|Siment Flower}}, it will still retain traces of magic.

## Magic Power and Sound Patterns

Created things are containers of magical energy; the so-called magical capacity refers to how much magical energy a created thing can store. As described above, magical energy has a tendency toward equilibrium. Therefore, the principle that "created things are containers of magical energy" actually means that created things have a higher magical potential. Thus, when they contain less magical energy, the magical energy from their surroundings will spontaneously flow into them. From this, we can see that magical capacity—i.e., how much magical energy can be stored—is mainly determined by the level of a created thing's magical potential (because once the magical energy concentration inside a created thing reaches a very high value, it forms equilibrium with the outside world, and the magical energy will still flow, but the inflow and outflow rates are equal).

所谓魔法使生来就具有更高的魔力量，一方面是由于他们生来就拥有一个相当高的魔力势（在成年之前完全形成），另一方面，魔法使可以自由控制魔力进出自己的身体，让魔力欠载，满载，甚至过载。这个能力带来了包括但不限于下述的许多好处：

- Can restore 魔力 extremely quickly, as if inexhaustible

- When necessary, a massive amount of 魔力 can be provided for a single spellcast through overload.

- Others find it difficult to perceive the existence of a 魔法使 through 魔力.

&nbsp;

Regarding the explanation of the third point: it is necessary to elaborate in detail on what magic power corresponding to the ether actually is. First, magic power possesses **divisibility**. This means you can understand magic power as an enormous number of extremely small fictional particles, like molecules, except without actual volume. This allows magic power to be arranged into certain structures by something specific, just like molecules. Without external forces, magic power that has been arranged into a certain structure tends to return to a state of disordered diffusion. In order to manipulate magic power, mages often arrange the magic power within their bodies according to personal habits (Manipulate). This arrangement method is roughly consistent across all intelligent beings—the closer a creation's {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d0-9e62-e87f3814a3ed|Phase}} is, the more similar their methods of arranging magic power tend to be. This method is like the genome of magic power, which we call a "voice pattern" (all creatures have genomes, and those of the same species are more similar, but each individual is unique) (if you don't understand what a genome is, just think of fingerprints). The act of identifying others by sensing magic power is actually accomplished by distinguishing the structured magic power that a creation emits when exchanging magic power with its surroundings. In casual conversation, this arrangement method may sometimes be directly referred to as "magic power." (e.g. {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81b5-98c3-cabe55174ae3|四季高岭之赐 其四}} "Ferdinand couldn't tell who the visitor was, but he could distinguish that non-human **magic power** from hundreds of meters away.") The "magic residue" mentioned in 辛德拉试验 and other similar statements actually refer to structured magic power; since magic power is ubiquitous, verifying whether magic power exists is as foolish as verifying whether there is air on Earth. The discrepancy between this "magic" and "voice pattern" is akin to the scientific information gap in the real world—if you understand science well enough, you can comprehend the origins of everyday phenomena as well as their academic names.

However, magic users don't need to arrange their 魔力 in order to wield it. Even the 魔力 that radiates from their bodies is disordered and scattered, which means it's very difficult for you to realize that this is 魔力 emanating from a 造物, and thus to become aware of the existence of other beings. Even if a 魔法使 is already very close to you (meaning you are already in an area with relatively high 魔力 around them), they can still prevent you from detecting them by suppressing the exchange of 魔力 between themselves and the outside world.

The name 声纹 comes from the experiment in which it was discovered, which used a 胁迫 魔力 vibration, then visualized the vibration into a texture.

## How do creations manipulate magic?

Created beings serve as containers for 魔力, though the distribution of 魔力 within these containers is not uniform. This distribution generally follows the principle of use it or lose it—concentrating most heavily at the extremities of the limbs, particularly the hands.
