Innate Magic, formally *Innatus*, is a form of magic unique to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d5-873e-fa7a17ab7dbb|Sorcerers}}  — a manifestation of their magical specificity.

Most Innate Magic is bound to bloodline. The classic example is {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-811c-bdb9-f23f6e564acc|Clan Elizabeth}}’s {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-814b-8023-ef813b1fe69f|Mnemonia}}  — which is easy enough to understand, since "magic flows in the blood of Sorcerers."
