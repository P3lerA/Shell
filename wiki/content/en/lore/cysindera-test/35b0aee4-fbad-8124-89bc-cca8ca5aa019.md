The Cysindera Test works just like a Luminol test for {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8126-aa93-c422b2f64b85|Mana}} (I hope you know what that is) — as long as the surface hasn't been treated with a Suppressant such as Siment Flower honey, even after decades, any **trace** residue of Mana will produce a bright yellow glow.
