At the beginning of the world's creation, there were no gods — only the creator, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8173-8769-d05e181f7439|Riven C. Majesty}}.

Since the story took root and the world grew lush and flourishing, consciousness divided, and Thrones appeared beyond the god of mystery. The Creator granted his authority to mortals, who thus ascended to godhood.
