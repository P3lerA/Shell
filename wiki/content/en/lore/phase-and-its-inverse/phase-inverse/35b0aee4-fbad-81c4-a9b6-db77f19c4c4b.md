Phase Inverse, that is, the inverse of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d0-9e62-e87f3814a3ed|Phase}}. Still using matrices as an example, a matrix's inverse matrix has the Phase Inverse of the original matrix's elements.

This explanation is clearly too abstract. The concepts of Phase and Phase Inverse originally stem from matter and antimatter. It can be said that if matter possesses a certain phase, the corresponding antimatter has the corresponding inverse phase. For things where a certain "phase" represents existence, when those possessing phase and inverse phase meet, they annihilate like matter and antimatter — for example, magical power. This is also the fundamental principle of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81bd-8a1f-e2732fbea28f|护盾术}}.
