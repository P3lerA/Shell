Memory is a kind of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8126-aa93-c422b2f64b85|Mana}}, forming information through orderly arrangement, just like your hard drive. From this perspective, all of Ragriff's creations are sorcerers.

Therefore, strictly speaking, no creation exists without 魔力. However, the amount of 魔力 that 记忆 itself possesses is several orders of magnitude lower than what is required to manipulate magic or be perceived, and it is practically impossible for it to be controlled by magic.
