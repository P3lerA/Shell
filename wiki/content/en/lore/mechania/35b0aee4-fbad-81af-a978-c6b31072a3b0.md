As mentioned in the {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8126-aa93-c422b2f64b85|Mana}} entry, 魔力 is not evenly distributed throughout a creation's body—for example, the hands have the most because they are constantly used for casting spells.

However, some psionic magic requires direct manipulation through the brain, so mages' brains generally contain relatively more 魔力. While this facilitates casting such magics, it also makes it possible for other mages to manipulate this 魔力 and thereby control others' minds or even their behavior. Through the long course of evolution, the creator developed a mechanism to counter this threat: Mechania.

&nbsp;

Biologically speaking, Mechania refers to the soft tissue on the inner side of the skull. This tissue is generally more developed in magicians. It blocks foreign 魔力 from passing through ("foreign" here means the voice pattern does not match that individual, see {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8126-aa93-c422b2f64b85|Mana}} ), making mental manipulation of others considerably more challenging.

Mechania can be actively practiced and strengthened, and this is also an important topic of discussion in 精神防御术.

&nbsp;

Sorcerers lack this structure, and this structure also cannot prevent sorcerers from manipulating others' minds, because sorcerers have no voiceprint (see {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8126-aa93-c422b2f64b85|Mana}} as before). This also makes it **almost** impossible to manipulate a sorcerer's mind.
