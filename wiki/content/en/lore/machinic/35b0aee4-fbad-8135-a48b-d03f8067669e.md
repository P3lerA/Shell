Similar to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a8-91df-e6fddbcedc3d|Mage & Sorcerers}} distinction, Machinics also differentiate from commonly assumed settings.

Medium for casting magic. It is worth noting that most magic can be cast barehanded without any Machinic, and some magic cannot use a Machinic at all.

Machinics — such as a wand — primarily serve to gather Mana and assist the caster in manipulating it.
