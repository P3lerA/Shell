Similar to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a8-91df-e6fddbcedc3d|Mage & Sorcerers}}, Machinics also have the distinction of being common knowledge settings.

The medium for casting magic. It is worth noting that most magic can be cast barehanded without any Machinic, though some magic cannot use a Machinic.

Machinic, such as magic staffs, primarily serve to gather magic and assist spell casters in manipulating magical power.
