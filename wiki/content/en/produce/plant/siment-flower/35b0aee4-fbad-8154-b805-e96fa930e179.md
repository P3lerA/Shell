Siment Flower can suppress the surrounding Mana; the larger the plant, the stronger this effect. It is an extremely rare and dangerous plant. Unlike {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-811f-813f-c0bc31ea3532|Ashfield Grass}}, which "cannot grow in places with Mana," Siment Flower actively makes magic difficult to use in its vicinity.

Additionally, the nectar of the Siment Flower is a high-performance type of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81b6-9751-e71b374377b5|Suppressant}}.
