Siment Flower, also known as 赛蒙特花, can suppress the surrounding magic; the larger the plant, the stronger this effect. It is an extremely rare and dangerous plant. Unlike {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-811f-813f-c0bc31ea3532|Ashfield Grass}}, which "cannot grow in places with magic," magic is difficult to use in places with magic.

Additionally, the nectar of the Siment Flower is a high-performance type of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81b6-9751-e71b374377b5|抑魔剂}}.
