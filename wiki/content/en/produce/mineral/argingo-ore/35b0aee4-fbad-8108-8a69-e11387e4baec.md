A mineral unique to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d3-9ec6-fdf9db1bba0f|Indigo Mountains}}. Argingo Ore itself appears silvery with a bluish tint, hard yet lightweight, and when encountering {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8130-abfc-f1faa945a04f|Demon}}, it emits a bright blue glow, making it an excellent material for crafting weapons and armor.
