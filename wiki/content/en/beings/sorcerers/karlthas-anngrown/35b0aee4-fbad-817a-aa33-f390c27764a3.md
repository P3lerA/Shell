Karlthas•Anngrown (3102-), mage, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8115-9d53-d76f5166f9af|Angro}}, has one-quarter {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8198-8b44-f104dfaeb276|Dragon}} blood.

In the Anngrown family, where dragon blood is everywhere, relatively few display magical talent. Karlthas is a prominent one. As a descendant of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8100-9d67-e3601abd5c31|Blue Dragon}}, he possesses quite reasonable innate magic: his gaze can gather magical power. (A dragon's gaze carries magical power)
