Tar Ramesses (1312-1375), human spellcaster, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8184-b84c-cea704d6240b|Sylvatia}} native, major contributor to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81b0-a0ee-eff11a1a5408|显形魔法}} research, co-founder of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8106-b3b9-c40d8dfd9446|SMA}}.

&nbsp;

During his school years, Tar Ramesses immediately demonstrated exceptional magical talent, and during his time at school completed important research on the simplification of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81b0-a0ee-eff11a1a5408|显形魔法}}. Everyone believed he would become an excellent mage remembered throughout history.

However, aside from some assistance with construction work by {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8106-b3b9-c40d8dfd9446|SMA}}, Tar contributed virtually nothing to the magical cause for the rest of his life.
