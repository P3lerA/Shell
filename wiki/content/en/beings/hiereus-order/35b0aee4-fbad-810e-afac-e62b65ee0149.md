A clan existing within the {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8167-9318-faaf646d532a|Elf}}s that divines destiny, revealing the guidance of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d9-a735-dc5e0d48876d|Mnemosyne}} and mastering the esoteric {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d5-a7b8-e40d8972d75e|Astreia}}.

Though few in number, they have produced many famous sorcerers.

It is worth noting that the Clan of Hiereus is merely a collective term in modern times. In the era when {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8171-bb00-edea89ed9092|Oraculum}} was still flourishing, the Clan of Hiereus was singular, but after the final division between {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d9-856a-db25779096a2|Common Elf}} and {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81e3-b34f-f38d0c851cf9|High Elf}}, the Common Elves established their own unique priest institution.
