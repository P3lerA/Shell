Arthur Bola (2886-2919), poet, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81c7-bef2-fa4750d2e5b7|Althea}} native, talented yet died young.

Bola, who came from a well-to-do family, did not wallow in pleasure. On the contrary, after meeting the love of his life, the two of them retired to a cabin at the edge of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81e6-b8e0-cf9dcd27c5e0|Cerulean Forest}}, where Bola devoted himself to composing poetry.

Happy lives are mostly alike, while each misfortune is unique. When his lover was humiliated by outsiders, the enraged Arthur Bola initiated a duel he was destined to lose.

The miracle did not happen, and thus a genius perished. But perhaps he still left us a legacy unknown to the world.
