Theodore Cysindera, the human branch of the Cysindera family, a politician who once lived in the southern New Continent, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-817f-acc0-fef894698c6a|The Nameless Prophet}}'s ancestor.

&nbsp;

Due to implementing overly radical reforms, he made numerous enemies in the parliament and was framed by the conservatives. His descendants went into exile to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d4-9e43-fe9f579084f6|SR}}; some sought political asylum from {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81f3-a40a-e697e59d813d|Ostia}}, while others settled in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8115-9d53-d76f5166f9af|Angro}}, which represents the ancestral line of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8186-91b8-fb2e1f37eb5a|Ferdinand C. Argos}}.
