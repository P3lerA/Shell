变形术 is a spell obtained by deconstructing {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-816a-882b-e83bf69e073d|塑形魔法}}, extracting and simplifying certain branches, invented by the great magician {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a5-bd26-c1ad84f777ae|Keaperra Thurandare}}.

&nbsp;

Simple and easy to use with powerful effects, 变形术 is a highly practical magic. Correspondingly, because it lacks the complete mechanism of 塑形 compared to 塑形魔法, 变形术 cannot target itself, and will impart the target with some of the properties of the shape being transformed into. Most of the time, these properties are drawbacks. *For example, transforming into a cow does not change the sense of taste, but one would have to eat grass.*

&nbsp;

Even so, the convenience brought by 变形术 led to its widespread dissemination. In 1995, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8163-a0d6-d76cd22fd2a2|M.A.R.}} classified 变形术 as a Level 3 dangerous magic, and it was upgraded to Level 2 in 2010.
