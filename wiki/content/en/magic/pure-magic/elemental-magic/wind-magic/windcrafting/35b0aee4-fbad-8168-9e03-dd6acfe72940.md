Magic that commands air to form wind, serving as the foundation for many wind magic spells.

&nbsp;

Notably, WindCrafting is generally used for relatively precise and small-scale operations; for large-scale purposes, such as making people levitate, one should use {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ff-aa27-d29f0fd945f3|//Gale Magic}}.
