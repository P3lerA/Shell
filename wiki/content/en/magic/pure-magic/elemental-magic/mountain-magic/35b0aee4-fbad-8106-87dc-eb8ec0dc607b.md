Even among elemental magic, Mountain Magic can be said to be quite special. After all, the definition of "mountain" is extremely broad, and the plains seen everywhere can be considered extensions of "mountains", so not only does Mountain Magic possess tremendous power, it can even be cast in most places without special catalysts — sounding almost like a strengthened version of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d1-b5c2-c481426c6dae|Wind Magic}}. Its drawbacks are practically self-evident: Mountain Magic is extremely precise and complex, often requiring unreasonable amounts of mana, and without the bloodline of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8142-bf13-f4676fc46608|Hillkind}}, it's not as easy to use as it might appear.
