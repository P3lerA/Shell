A magic invented by {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8128-8715-cd9a07205d74|Versatilys Sellano}}, which was continuously improved through research by {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8106-b3b9-c40d8dfd9446|SMA}}.

Magic that creates {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81c4-a9b6-db77f19c4c4b|Phase Inverse}} magical power and forms a structured pattern. Unlike {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81bd-8a1f-e2732fbea28f|护盾术}}, 障壁魔法 distributes incoming impacts evenly like an arch bridge when struck, thereby achieving far superior high-density attack defense performance compared to 护盾术.

The disadvantage of this approach is that 障壁魔法 cannot wrap protective magic around the user like 护盾术 does. This is because the magic that constitutes a barrier has a structured pattern, causing barriers to necessarily take on regular geometric shapes (refer to the principle of crystals).
