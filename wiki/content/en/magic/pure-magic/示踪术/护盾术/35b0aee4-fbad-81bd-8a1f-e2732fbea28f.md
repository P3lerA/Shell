A spell that creates a {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81c4-a9b6-db77f19c4c4b|Phase Inverse}} mana cluster, which can be used to block mana.

There's no particular technique involved—simply coating your surroundings with mana forms a barrier. Shield Magic is an extremely basic yet practical defensive spell. Correspondingly, its fatal flaw is that when faced with high-density magic attacks, Shield Magic's barrier can be easily pierced, becoming a Maginot Line. {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81e4-84b3-d119ce81e01f|障壁魔法}} was invented to improve upon this flaw.
