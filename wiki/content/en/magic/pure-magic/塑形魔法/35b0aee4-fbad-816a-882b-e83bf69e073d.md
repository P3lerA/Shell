Complex and precise magic with an extremely high learning cost. The origin of most {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8126-aae2-c56448bca4ef|变形术}}.

&nbsp;

As its name suggests, the function of 塑形魔法 is to "shape" the appearance of something. Unlike 变形术, 塑形魔法 **only changes the target's appearance.** Additionally, 塑形魔法 can target oneself as the spell's recipient, whereas 变形术 cannot.
