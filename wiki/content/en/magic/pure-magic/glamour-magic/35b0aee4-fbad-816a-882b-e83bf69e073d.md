Complex and precise magic with an extremely high learning cost. The origin of most {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8126-aae2-c56448bca4ef|//Transmutation}}.

&nbsp;

As its name suggests, the function of Glamour Magic is to "shape" the appearance of something. Unlike Transmutation, Glamour Magic **only changes the target's appearance.** Additionally, Glamour Magic can target oneself as the spell's recipient, while Transmutation cannot.
