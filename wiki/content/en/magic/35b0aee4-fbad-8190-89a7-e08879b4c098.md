A supernatural force that has penned countless tales. Within the vast majority of creations exists {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8126-aa93-c422b2f64b85|Mana}}, and through some form of directing this power, magic is formed.

Magic is mainly divided into two categories: Pure Magic and Elemental Magic. As the name suggests, the former harnesses pure Mana, while the latter tends to use Mana to manipulate the {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-813b-aa3f-ed7ddd07602f|Four Elements}}—water, fire, wind, and mountains.
