The four great elements that constitute **existence** are: Wind, Mountain, Water, and Fire. Elemental Magic is the practice of wielding these four elements through magic, forming the four corresponding types of magic.

For a long time, elemental magic was overlooked by the major magic associations due to its lack of practicality, until the ArchMage {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a5-bd26-c1ad84f777ae|Keaperra Thurandare}} established a systematic elemental magic system, making it more accessible and more feasible.
