Compared to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d1-b5c2-c481426c6dae|Wind Magic}} and {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8106-87dc-eb8ec0dc607b|Mountain Magic}}, it reads as a more standard elemental magic of Ragriff, requiring fire or a fire source to cast. Even so, there has never been a shortage of people obsessed with it in the world —after all, who could fail to be moved by the raging infernos kindled by your own hands?
