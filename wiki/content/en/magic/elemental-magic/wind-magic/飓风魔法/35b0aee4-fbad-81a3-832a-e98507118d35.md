A brilliant creation combining {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ff-aa27-d29f0fd945f3|风场魔法}} and {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8168-9e03-dd6acfe72940|塑风魔法}}, invented by the great mage {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a5-bd26-c1ad84f777ae|Keaperra Thurandare}}.

The effects of 飓风魔法 are exactly as the name suggests — magic that uses 魔力 to conjure hurricanes. Its genius lies in combining the massive scale of 风场魔法 with the precise controllability of 塑风魔法, annihilating anyone who dares to oppose it in the raging winds.
