The magic that to fly wielding the wind.

&nbsp;

To avoid any possible ambiguity, note: magic such as {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-816f-be76-d39b6c108b61|Fireball}} requires fire to use, can only be used in wind, and cannot fly freely — only glide with the wind. Before {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ff-aa27-d29f0fd945f3|Gale Magic}} was invented, the limitations were significant.

Only after Gale Magic was invented did it become possible to use the Flight Magic by borrowing wind fields. For details, see {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ff-aa27-d29f0fd945f3|Gale Magic}}.
