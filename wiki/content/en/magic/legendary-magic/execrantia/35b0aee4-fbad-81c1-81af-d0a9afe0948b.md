An ancient magic for imposing {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ca-af7a-c10dd250d4e7|Curse}} upon a target, invented by {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-810b-bfc0-c7412c6c5109|Daemon R. Brigid}} in the primordial age.

&nbsp;

Execrantia that directly manipulates fate is one of the most dangerous magics in the world, its danger even exceeding {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-814b-8023-ef813b1fe69f|Mnemonia}}. Fortunately, since Brigid ascended to godhood, he has rarely appeared among people, and this magic has hardly been disseminated.
