A unique magic learned by elves from the blessing of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8171-bb00-edea89ed9092|Oraculum}}, which allows elves to communicate with forests.

{{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81be-a414-f64539393cb8|Wood Elf}} are particularly skilled at this type of magic, and can even use the manipulation of plant roots and branches for attack and defense.
