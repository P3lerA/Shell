A magic unique to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81e3-aca6-cbb05a977af1|Mirth}} that requires no casting medium. It can beguile the minds of intelligent creatures whose magical power is weaker than its own, causing them to obey the caster's every command.

&nbsp;

Notably, even if the casting target possesses greater magical power, countering mirthery still requires actively deploying a defense. Given that mirth tend to appear in swarms, this can sometimes be a rather tricky form of magic.
