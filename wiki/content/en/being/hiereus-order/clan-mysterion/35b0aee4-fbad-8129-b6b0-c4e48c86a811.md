A priestly clan of the {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d9-856a-db25779096a2|Common Elf}}, recent but scholarly in spirit. After {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81c7-bef2-fa4750d2e5b7|Althea}} was established, the Common Elves chose the Grey elf clan of Mysterion to reveal guidance. Mysterion means *secret rites*.

Since being chosen, Clan Mysterion has contributed significantly to the research of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d5-a7b8-e40d8972d75e|Astreia}}. The prophecies they have revealed are also more concise and understandable compared to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81cc-b59d-f41ef4dca80f|Clan Hiereus}}.
