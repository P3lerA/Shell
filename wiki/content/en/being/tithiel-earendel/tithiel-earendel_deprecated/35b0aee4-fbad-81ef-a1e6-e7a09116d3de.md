Tithiel Earendel (3120-), a {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-818d-956e-e2a7a7b1329d|Selene}} person, a girl with one-eighth elven blood, raised in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81c7-bef2-fa4750d2e5b7|Althea}}.

&nbsp;

At age eleven, Tithiel’s father died from political persecution, and her mother fled with her to the elven lands seeking refuge. However, the arrogant high elves refused to aid the descendants of the grey elves, and at {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-812b-99e0-f070d197bd7b|Lake Elf}} lakeside, Tithiel, who had witnessed her mother's assassination, had no choice but to continue fleeing northward.

Finally, when Tithiel came to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8109-8d46-e8c4fe6e5692|July Archipelago}}, a tolerant couple of gray elves adopted their distant relative, and she received her education and grew up there.

&nbsp;

Tithiel ‘s rapier Crínisus was a gift from her adoptive father when she set out on her journey.
