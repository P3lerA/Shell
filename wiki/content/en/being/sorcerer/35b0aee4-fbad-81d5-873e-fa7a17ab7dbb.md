Magic is an innate talent of Sorcerers, and they can cast magics without mediums such as wand, because it flows within their blood.

Such talents often originate from a certain bloodline, for example, the magical users in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-811c-bdb9-f23f6e564acc|Clan Elizabeth}} often possess {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-814b-8023-ef813b1fe69f|Mnemonia}} as an {{link:35c0aee4-fbad-80d0-8686-f8520f07f261|Innate Magic}}.
