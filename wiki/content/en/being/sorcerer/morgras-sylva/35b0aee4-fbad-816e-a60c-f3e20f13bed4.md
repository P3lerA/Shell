Morgras Sylva (1384 – 1455), {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8143-b3fa-e5f3c11e7f08|Fallax}} Sorcerer, descendant of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8184-b455-cefd09344ba5|Elvin Sylva}}, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8184-b84c-cea704d6240b|Sylvatia}} native. All of her descendants bear the middle name "Morgras" — perhaps an attempt to convey some kind of will.
