A small-bodied flying species, agile and noisy, with some intelligence and magical ability. They can speak Common, although they don't particularly enjoy doing so. They possess a unique magic of the same name, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8111-99d4-e91767b0879d|Mirthery}}, capable of enchanting the minds of intelligent creatures.

&nbsp;

Excluding subspecies, it is found almost exclusively in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8140-bae9-c76ca8d09a08|Forest Bartow}}.
