A subspecies of Mirth, born from pure malice. Without using {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81b0-a0ee-eff11a1a5408|显形魔法}} to force the exposure of its shattered body, there is no significant difference in appearance between Spite and Mirth.

&nbsp;

In the midst of long-standing hatred, they have forgotten how to use {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8111-99d4-e91767b0879d|Mirthery}} and instead evolved {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8198-ac6f-f94810d52719|Hex}}.

&nbsp;

Before {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8100-abef-d61c68b42d4c|Sunglow Tower}} was inverted, it was only visible in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a1-8da1-ddc6c9e4f993|Detholock}}. Afterward, it became visible within {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8144-b705-c03ba8df8fdd|NR}}.
