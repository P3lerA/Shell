Small in stature, with exaggeratedly pointed and long ears and grayish-brown skin, this is a cunning species. Their name, Fallax, means *the deceitful*.

&nbsp;

Like the {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8139-9757-ecc1dafe1446|Dwarf}}, the Fallax is also proficient in smithing, but in a distinctly different manner.

Fallax are not particularly skilled at using {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8108-8a69-e11387e4baec|Argingo Ore}}, such a beautiful mineral, to forge exquisite weapons. The strength of Fallax weapons often lies in certain special properties, which are endowed by Fallax's unique magical system.

&nbsp;

Although Fallax are often cunning and deceitful, they possess a strong sense of contract, considering it unforgivable to break a contract.

Fallax mostly inhabit {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8184-b84c-cea704d6240b|Sylvatia}}, though their figures can sometimes be seen in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8144-b705-c03ba8df8fdd|NR}} as well.
