A giant humanoid species living scattered in the northeastern corner of the world ({{link:35b0aee4-fbad-817c-86bf-c4033f9eb7ea|Gap place}}, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8191-8132-f6b8d998d8de|Sunglow Archipelago}}), with heights between 5-15 meters and significant variation between individuals.

They have no specific language, but can speak some simple {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a0-be0f-ff62f4ed24ba|Lingua-Franca}}.
