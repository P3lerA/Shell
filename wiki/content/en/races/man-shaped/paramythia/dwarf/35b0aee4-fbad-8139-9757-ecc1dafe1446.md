A short, stocky race, the Dwarves mainly inhabit {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81f3-a40a-e697e59d813d|Ostia}}. They are always bearded and irritable. Unlike other races who enjoy basking in the sun, Dwarves prefer to dig for endless wealth underground.

&nbsp;

Dwarves are masters of forging, possessing certain forge magic unknown even to elves, and they mine a peculiar metal known as {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8108-8a69-e11387e4baec|Argingo Ore}}.
