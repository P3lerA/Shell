Elves are an elegant, pure, and arrogant race, possessing lifespans two times or even several times longer than those of humans.

&nbsp;

The first magic flows through the veins of Elves, which is why most magic users are Elves.

&nbsp;

Elves originated from the wasteland now known as the Pale Land, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-819e-8714-d1d33be9a203|Eltarnern}}, and after {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-813f-99bd-d0a2ab1ac81e|The Pale Night}}, they migrated northward until reaching the northernmost coast of southern Ragriff.
