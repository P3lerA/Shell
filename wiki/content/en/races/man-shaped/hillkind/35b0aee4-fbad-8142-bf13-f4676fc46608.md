Race that has dwelt in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-812a-9008-f5805e43ea39|Hillkind Mountains}} since ancient times, one of Ragriff's oldest creations. Hillkind communicate with each other using {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8125-b9fb-cb07f7c47662|Sindamm}}.

&nbsp;

Hillkind's physical form is generally no different from the humans of the northern nations, but the males are taller. Compared to humans, Hillkind have a pale grayish-blue skin, and hair that tends toward gray-white. The longer a Hillkind lives, the deeper the grayish-blue of their skin becomes. These characteristics are actually the result of a curse, the source of which can be found in their {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81e5-a23f-c28ec83130c0|Origin}}.

&nbsp;

Renowned for their mastery of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8106-87dc-eb8ec0dc607b|Mountain Magic}}, or rather, quite a lot of Hill Magic originated from Hillkind research.
