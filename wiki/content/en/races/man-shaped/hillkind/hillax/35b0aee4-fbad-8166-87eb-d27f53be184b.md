A subspecies derived from the Hillkind, the embodiment of a harsh curse. It originally originated as a punishment from {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8173-8769-d05e181f7439|Riven C. Majesty}} against the Glorat clan's transgression.

Hillaxes have darker skin than ordinary Hillkinds, and both their physique and lifespan are far inferior to those of their former fellow kin.

Perhaps because of every race's instinct to exclude those who are different, the descendants of the brave are instead discriminated against by some ordinary Hillkinds.
