**Errans:** Latin, wandering, lost.

**Planos** (πλάνος) — wandering/straying. Sounds similar to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81cd-962d-d1261b77d3b5|the Peírama}}.

{{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81cd-962d-d1261b77d3b5|the Peírama}}'s descendants, who originally advocated for staying among their people, remain as arrogant, as enigmatic as ever, still possessing the finest sorcerers in Ragriff — but no longer perfect.

Pale, silent, unsettling — such is the common trait of all ancient peoples. Unlike {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-817a-9606-e43018048fcd|Ulter}}, the Planos seem convinced they can still recreate Peírama's glory — perhaps for them, pausing to look back like Ulter would be the gentler choice.
