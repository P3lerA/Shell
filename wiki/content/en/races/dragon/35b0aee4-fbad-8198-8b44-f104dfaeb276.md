A four-legged, two-winged flying species—the romantic essence of Western fantasy.

In Ragriff, Dragons are not such a common species, but people won't be surprised to see a Dragon in a tavern either.

Dragons have relatively long lifespans. Without specific notation (such as {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-817f-89cf-dcd69ae3a3a3|Fire Dragon}}, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8100-9d67-e3601abd5c31|Blue Dragon}}, or other common subspecies), lifespans generally reach 200-500 years, while individual long-lived species (such as {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8135-afa4-e9535013feb4|Seawyrm}}) can even reach several thousand years.

Unless otherwise noted, Dragons cannot communicate using human language. A "Dragon Speaker" who can communicate with Dragons is a rare talent.

&nbsp;

Dragons often perceive prolonged eye contact as an unfriendly act.
