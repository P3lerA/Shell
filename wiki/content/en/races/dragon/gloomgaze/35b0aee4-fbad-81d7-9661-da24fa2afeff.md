A dangerous species with powerful magic, adept at various {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8153-99f4-e6e7349a7f02|龙视魔法}}, and because their eyes are dim and their eye sockets sunken, making it difficult to discern what they are gazing at, they are excellent hunters.

&nbsp;

Commonly found in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8197-8ef4-c38aa8d43b86|Mount Angro}}, with small amounts also visible in the southwest of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8107-8d84-fe317ecf0276|SingLand}}'s homeland.
