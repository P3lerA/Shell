Dragon whose wings are split into two at the tail end, hence the name, with relatively strong flight capability and relatively weak magical power.

It has a very docile temperament and is the only subspecies to have been domesticated on a large scale. It has a close relationship with {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8189-b0e7-c63e9858c6ba|Clan Anngrown}}, serving as a mount for the clan's senior members.

&nbsp;

Commonly found in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8197-8ef4-c38aa8d43b86|Mount Angro}}, and can be seen in many places.
