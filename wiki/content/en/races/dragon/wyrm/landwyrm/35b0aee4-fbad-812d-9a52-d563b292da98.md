A gentle giant herbivore species, with adults measuring 30–45 meters in body length and standing 20–35 meters in height.

&nbsp;

Occasionally found in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ff-826c-ef19be4a0ca3|Coimbra Archipelago}} and the southeast of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81e7-ba77-cf3f552bdc77|Pandemoniac}}.
