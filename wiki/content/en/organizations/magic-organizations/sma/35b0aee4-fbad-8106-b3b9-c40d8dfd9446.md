Southern Magic Association (hereinafter SMA), like {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8163-a0d6-d76cd22fd2a2|M.A.R.}}, is an association aimed at popularizing and standardizing magic.

The difference is that SMA was originally a small magical research organization founded by {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8128-8715-cd9a07205d74|Versatilys Sellano}} in 1353, which, under the former's political leadership, gradually developed over several decades into an entity that directs the magical development of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8184-b84c-cea704d6240b|Sylvatia}} and {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81fc-96e1-cb45c169a26d|Lukatýsrika}}.

&nbsp;

As mentioned above, SMA's primary administrative targets are countries and powers in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8184-b84c-cea704d6240b|Sylvatia}} and {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81fc-96e1-cb45c169a26d|Lukatýsrika}}, with the following primary responsibilities:

&nbsp;

1. Research and Optimization of Magic

2. {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8179-8c7e-c8d07b7586ff|Management of Dangerous Magic}} 

3. The Spread and Popularization of Magic

&nbsp;

SMA was initially established in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81bc-baef-cf3413a39925|Zeska}}. In 1363, Versatilys Sellano, seeking to escape political constraints and for various other considerations, decided to relocate the headquarters to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ea-ab50-f4603d14b426|Puteoli}}.

Today, the headquarters of SMA remains in Puteoli, and it also has a branch in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81fc-96e1-cb45c169a26d|Lukatýsrika}}.
