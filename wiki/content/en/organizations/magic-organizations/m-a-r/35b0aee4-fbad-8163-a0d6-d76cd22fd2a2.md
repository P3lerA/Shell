The Magic Association of Ragriff, aka the MARCH Association (referred to as the M.A.R. below), was established by the ArchMage {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8145-a0d7-de7b90d7a205|Ryne Lynne}} in 1230 in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-818b-a71e-cba6e166d7f7|Singhui}}, with the purpose of popularizing and standardizing magic.

&nbsp;

The regulatory target of M.A.R. is the countries and forces within {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-811b-8402-e33e2e5244ea|Ragriff}}'s territory except {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ff-a59a-e8e1abcb1e31|Fidel}}. Its main duties are as follows:

1. Research and Optimization of Magic

2. {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8179-8c7e-c8d07b7586ff|Management of Dangerous Magic}}

3. The Spread and Popularization of Magic

4. Final Determination of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8129-9d52-fe1a37949be0|ArchMages}}

&nbsp;

M.A.R.'s headquarters to this day remains where it was established, Singhui. Additionally, the association has branches in most regions of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-811b-8402-e33e2e5244ea|Ragriff}}. They are located in: {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-812a-9008-f5805e43ea39|Hillkind Mountains}}, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8133-8aba-c1866d131470|South Alliance}}, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-817c-86bf-c4033f9eb7ea|Gap place}}, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8115-9d53-d76f5166f9af|Angro}}, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81f3-a40a-e697e59d813d|Ostia}}, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-818d-956e-e2a7a7b1329d|Selene}}.

&nbsp;

*Notably, *{{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d5-873e-fa7a17ab7dbb|Sorcerers}}* do not fall under M.A.R.'s jurisdiction.*
