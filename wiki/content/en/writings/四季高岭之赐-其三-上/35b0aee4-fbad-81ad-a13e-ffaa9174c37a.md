Decades after the attack, out of curiosity, 四季高岭 took control of 希诺蒙, and this control lasted longer than the combined total of all her previous exertions over others.

Professor Shinomon would often realize that he was being controlled by something beyond his own will. Initially, he thought it was some kind of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81ae-ac61-d26317315f86|傀儡术}}. When this control became more and more frequent and completely impossible to resist, this seasoned magician realized it was a force beyond his own comprehension.

&nbsp;

He told almost no one the reason why, but Professor Shinom resigned from his job and began traveling north. He wanted to satisfy his wish before losing control of this body: to move to {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8107-8d84-fe317ecf0276|SingLand}}, the place he had longed for as a boy. Unfortunately, even when the soul known as Shinom Laplace dissipated, his body had never once set foot in SingLand. — Shijigaoling went there once, and she didn't much like it.

&nbsp;

He lingered on like this, enduring over a hundred years of suffering. Everything happened. The rise of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8136-9faf-c037fd931a68|Argos}}, {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-818d-80c3-c1c7f0b0045f|ALLGOES}}'s ascension to godhood, war, creation, destruction. Then in 3140, Shiki Kōryū dragged that broken body of his and knocked on the door of the wooden cabin.
