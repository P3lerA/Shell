A historical novel about the turbulent political situation of Selene a decade ago, containing detailed accounts of numerous political secrets of that nation. The author is the renowned New Continent journalist Xu Zhongyun, and this is the third volume.

The front page bears the inscription "国立第一图书馆", and the notes in the book have greatly increased compared to the first two volumes, with handwriting that is sloppy and hard to decipher.

Looking closely, you can see the faint traces of tears scattered across the yellowed pages.
