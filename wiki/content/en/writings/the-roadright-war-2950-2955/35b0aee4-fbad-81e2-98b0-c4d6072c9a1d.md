A book written in the Common Tongue on thin, light paper, which details the entire course of the First 欧戈斯-山臣 War. The paper has yellowed severely. The author's name has been deliberately erased.

---

&nbsp;

In this layer of historical past, the Ogos people ultimately achieved tremendous success. The only price they paid for their previous ambiguous behavior was even greater territory. Shan Chen, due to rash advance, suffered a major defeat; the stakes even devoured the greatest one in their ranks.

&nbsp;

---

This unnamed author mentions that 俄瑞亚's disciples hesitated about their identity, with one of them (whose name was deliberately blackened) even returning to 欧戈斯 to organize an army, while the other simply stood by from the beginning.
