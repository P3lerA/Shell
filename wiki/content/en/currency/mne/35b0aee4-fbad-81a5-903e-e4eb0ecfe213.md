Since the elves appeared in the world, it has been a widely used ancient currency. Its name comes from the blessing of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81d9-a735-dc5e0d48876d|Mnemosyne}}. Even in today's world where {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-810c-ab8d-c2a33dde4550|Sel}} is popular, Mne remains the most important currency within elven territories.

Shaped like a circle, the silver coin features the image of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8171-bb00-edea89ed9092|Oraculum}} carved in the center with intricate Elven craftsmanship.
