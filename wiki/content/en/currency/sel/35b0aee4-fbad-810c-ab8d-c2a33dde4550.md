The continent's standard currency, found everywhere.

Once it was only the official currency of {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8107-8d84-fe317ecf0276|SingLand}}, and after {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8112-81cf-d799fd888d78|Tiyo Settyuu}}, a SingLand person, ascended to divinity, it was promoted to a lingua franca currency, now uniformly issued by the governments of SingLand and {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-8184-b84c-cea704d6240b|Sylvatia}}.

It is a circular coin of mixed gold and copper, adorned on top with a sun pattern, and below it the word Sel is inscribed in {{link:35b0aee4-fbad-81a0-be0f-ff62f4ed24ba|Lingua-Franca}}.
